🏠 Welcome to GarbageSort+

GarbageSort+ empowers communities to manage waste responsibly. Using machine learning, it classifies garbage into categories like cardboard, paper, glass, metal, plastic, and trash, providing a real-time eco-score.

Our aim is to make waste management smarter and sustainable using cutting-edge AI tools. Whether you are a student, a researcher, or a policymaker, GarbageSort+ can aid your recycling mission.

ℹ️ About GarbageSort+

GarbageSort+ is a smart web-based waste classifier that leverages TensorFlow and ConvNeXt-based neural networks. Users upload an image of a waste item, and the model predicts the type of waste and its eco-score.

Eco-scores are designed to guide users about the recyclability and environmental impact of the waste. This platform is intended for students, researchers, and civic authorities aiming to create sustainable communities.

Developed as part of an advanced internship project, it bridges AI with environmental conservation in a user-friendly interface.

📝 Rules and Guidelines

  • ✅ Upload images of only one item at a time for better accuracy.
  • 📸 Ensure the item is clearly visible and centered.
  • ❌ Avoid using offensive or copyrighted content.
  • 📚 Use this application responsibly and for educational or civic purposes.
  • 👤 Respect privacy policies and community guidelines.

♻️ Recycling Ideas

  • 🍵 Reuse glass jars as spice containers, planters or fairy light lanterns.
  • 📑 Turn newspaper into papier-mâché crafts, wall art or biodegradable seed pots.
  • 🛡️ Repurpose metal cans into lanterns, pencil holders, or hanging herb gardens.
  • 📢 Organize a local recycling campaign or DIY waste art competition.

  • 📖 Conduct workshops on reusability and upcycling techniques.

🔒 Permissions & Privacy

GarbageSort+ does not store your images or personal data. All uploaded images are processed securely and immediately discarded. We encourage transparency and welcome contributions or audits.

By using this app, you agree to use it ethically and respect open-source contributions. Avoid uploading sensitive, personal, or illegal content.
